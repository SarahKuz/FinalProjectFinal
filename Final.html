Preparing for The Storm

Can NYC survive another Sandy?

Its been nearly 6 years since Super Storm Sandy hit New York

Sandy revealed that much of NYC's infastructure is vulnerable to rising waters and future storms. As the negative effects of climate change continue to plauge our coast lines, one would expect NYC to halt or mitigate its waterfront developments. However, our most low-lying waterfronts, such as Red Hook and Lower-Manhattan, have continued to see massive amounts of development.
